# Mathematics Arena — STEM Games 2026

**Result: 1st place.**

## The task — *"Bots and how to catch them"*

Teams were asked to pick a specific kind of internet bot and design a method
to detect it (and, at least locally, remove it). The deliverable was a PDF
explaining the detection method together with a working implementation.

The official assignment is in [`task-description/`](task-description/).
