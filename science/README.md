# Science Arena — STEM Games 2026

This folder collects the ETF Sarajevo team's materials for the Science arena
of STEM Games 2026.

## Acknowledgement

The Science arena task description and the accompanying reference papers were
kindly provided by the **Odsjek za Fiziku, Univerzitet u Osijeku**
(Department of Physics, University of Osijek). We are sincerely grateful to
them for sharing these materials — it makes this archive more complete and
gives future ETF Sarajevo teams a fuller picture of what STEM Games involves.
Thank you.
